    • SUSTAINABILITY

    Reuse, refurbish, reimagine

    A refurbishment that enhances the building specification and improves functionality and user experience while limiting its Carbon impact.

    This consciousness in construction extends through every element of the campus, sowing the seeds for a future that benefits the climate and the community. Electric-only power minimises carbon footprint. Efficient and user-optimised light, heating and cooling technology minimises spending and sets the foundation for a spirit of wellbeing, bolstered by green and social spaces for a host of natural benefits.

    24 % REDUCTION

    in whole life carbon emissions compared to a new build scenario.

    Target accreditations:

    The economically and environmentally sound choice.

    GREENER LIVING

    A 10% net gain in biodiversity across the campus. The Loop, on-site gym, cycle storage and outdoor space encourage activity.

    RENEWABLE & RESILIENT

    Electric-only power that runs on a tariff using only renewable energy and future-proof innovative tech.

    ENERGY EFFICIENT

    Sustainable technology keep the required power resources to a minimum.

    ELEMENTAL DESIGN

    The open atrium and staircase design promotes active air circulation and strategic planting allows for further connection to nature.

    LOW IMPACT

    Buildings were refurbished not demolished, reducing carbon emissions and construction-related waste.
